PTE (Pearson Test of English)

Pearson Test of English Academic is a computer-based academic English language test aimed at non-native English speakers who desires to study abroad. It is designed to reward positive achievement in English language learning. The tests are mainly of Reading, Writing, Listening and Speaking.

The test is acknowledged by the UK Border Agency (UKBA) and Australian DIAC for student visa. The whole test is done in a single session in the time frame of 3 hours and is taken on computer in a secured test environment. The Speaking part of the exam is also done on the computer. The voice is recorded and sent for marking. The results are delivered within 5 days.

Content

Authentic and Academic test content from a variety of international contexts and scenarios.

Duration

Approximately 3 hours

Availability

PTE Academic is available in 43 territories including China, India, the US, Japan, South Korea, Australia, the UK, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Canada.
