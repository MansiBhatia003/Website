SAT(Scholastic Assessment Test)

SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test) evaluates the Mathematics, Reading, and Writing skills of students aspiring to pursue undergraduate studies abroad. This test is widely used as a parameter in the universities of the United States of America. SAT is a paper-based test which takes 3 hours to complete with an additional 50 more minutes for the essay.

The SAT score alone cannot guarantee student’s admission into a school – the test is only one of the important factors taken into consideration in the long process of an applicant getting admitted into the desired graduate school.
